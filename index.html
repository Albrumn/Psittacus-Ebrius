Iter Incipit — The Journey Begins

March 9, 2023

This site was lauched today to chronical the production of Psittacus Ebrius, a Latin language learning site. However, its inception occurred over a month ago.

After completing the short, fourteen-unit Duolingo Latin course back in January, I had a discussion with its former Trailblazer about the prospect of the course being extended. It wasn't promising. But this sparked an idea: why not develop my own online Latin course?

Others in the chat quickly began making suggestions.

Over the weekend, a good friend asked me about the progress I've made on Psittacus Ebrius. When I told her I hadn't made any, she said, "Really? Nothing? Why not?" This "why not" stuck with me, and I decided something is better than nothing. So, today I lauched a GitHub repo and a production blog for this project.

Will this vision ever become a reality? Only time will tell. But, hey, at least I started.
